ABOUT US

ACES is a licensed electrical contractor based in Fairfax, VA. We have proudly served the DC Metro area for over 25 years, bringing expertise, reliability, and top-tier craftsmanship to every project.

Our team of highly skilled electricians are equipped to handle projects of all sizes across DC, VA, and MD, ensuring quality execution from start to finish. We go beyond meeting industry stardards - We exceed them, prioritizing safety, quality, and trust in everything we do.

Building with Excellence

ACES is supported by an exceptionally skilled and deeply committed team that strives for excellence in everything we do for our customers, ourselves, and our craft. We are always looking for opportunities to improve and maximize each project, delivering the best electrical solutions for our customers.

Our Commitments

At ACES Electrical Services LLC, we partner with general contractors to ensure every project is executed with precision, efficiency, and excellence, exceeding client expectations every time.
