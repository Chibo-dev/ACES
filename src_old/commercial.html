COMMERCIAL SERVICES

At ACES, we deliver top-quality electrical services with Expert Project Management and a strong focus on Safety First. Our team provides Innovative Solutions. We pride ourselves on being Responsive to your needs and maintaining a Positive Reputation. Plus, we're fully Insured and Bonded for your peace of mind.

We have a comprehensive knowledge of new technologies, as well as systems that have been installed for years.
​Our reputation is one of integrity and responsiveness to customer needs no matter how large or small the project.

We Offer Full Commercial Electrical Services:

  • Retail & Commercial Tenant Build-Outs
  • Restaurants, Tenant Fit Ups, Fitness Center
  • Electrical Installations for New Construction.
  • Lighting Ballast Replacements/ Re-lamping
  • Energy Management & Efficiency
  • Maintenance, Troubleshooting, and Repairs
  • Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Stations
  • Electrical Safety Inspections
  • Installation & Maintenance of Parking lot lights
  • Transformer
  • Generators & ATS
  • Site-light installation including concrete bases and trench.
  • Underground Power Installations
  • Low Voltage (Voice/Data Cabling, CCTV, and Sound Systems)
  • Fire Alarm/Life Safe
