ADMINISTRATIVE

Our office provides diverse opportunities across Administrative, Accounting, Estimating, and Project Management departments. Responsible for overseeing contracts, compliance, scheduling, and communication, ensuring operational efficiency and project success while contributing to the company's overall growth and sustainability.

APPRENTICE ELECTRICIAN

The Apprentice electrician's responsibilities are to assist licensed electricians in installing, operating, maintaining, and repairing electrical systems to ensure safe and efficient performance.

JOURNEYMAN ELECTRICIAN

A Journeyman Electrician is a certified professional specializing in electrical systems, ensuring safety, efficiency, and compliance with industry regulations. They interpret blueprints, determine the best installation methods, and coordinate with supervisors, foremen, project managers, and contractors to ensure efficient workflow. Strong communication skills are essential for collaborating with teams.

FOREMAN

A Foreman is a skilled and experienced leader responsible for supervising and coordinating electrical projects on-site. They oversee journeyman electricians and apprentices, ensuring work is completed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with project specifications and regulations. Foremen interpret blueprints, assign tasks, manage schedules, and communicate with project managers, contractors, and clients to maintain workflow and project quality. Strong leadership, problem-solving, and communication skills are essential for success in this role.

SUPERVISOR

A Supervisor in the electrical field oversees and manages multiple projects, ensuring work is completed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with industry regulations. They coordinate between foremen, project managers, and contractors, monitor job progress, enforce safety standards, and provide technical guidance. Supervisors also handle scheduling, resource allocation, and problem-solving to maintain workflow and project quality. Strong leadership, communication, and organizational skills are essential for this role.
