ABOUT US

ACES is a licensed electrical contractor based in Fairfax, VA. We have proudly served the DC Metro area for over 25 years, bringing expertise, reliability, and top-tier craftsmanship to every project.

Our team of highly skilled electricians is equipped to handle projects of all sizes across DC, VA, and MD, ensuring quality execution from start to finish. We don't just meet industry standards — we exceed them, prioritizing safety, quality, and trust in everything we do.

Building with Excellence

ACES is powered by an exceptionally skilled and deeply committed team that strives for excellence in everything we do — for our customers, our craft, and our communities. We continuously look for opportunities to improve and add value to every project, delivering the best electrical solutions our clients deserve.

Our Commitments

At ACES Electrical Services LLC, we partner with general contractors and project owners to ensure every project is executed with precision, efficiency, and excellence — exceeding client expectations every time.

Our Values

Dedication to Our Workforce:

Our team is our greatest asset, and we deeply value every member of the ACES family. They are the cornerstone of our success on every project. We are dedicated to supporting our team and the communities we serve, striving to excel in every job we undertake. Our commitment extends beyond our projects, reflecting our dedication to the regions where we live and work.

Delivering Excellence Through Premium Resources:

We understand that achieving exceptional results requires top-notch resources and tools. That’s why we ensure that every element involved in your project—be it our equipment, skilled workforce, or any other necessary support—is of the highest quality. The safety of our team and clients is our top priority. We are dedicated to fostering a secure environment by implementing comprehensive safety programs, procedures, and training. Our commitment extends to ensuring that everyone involved feels protected and supported throughout every project.
